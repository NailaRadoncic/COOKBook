Spaghetti Carbonara

A creamy and delicious Italian pasta dish with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and black pepper.

Recipe Details

  • Cooking Time: 30 minutes
  • Servings: 4 people
  • Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • 200g Spaghetti
  • 100g Pancetta
  • 2 Large Eggs
  • 50g Parmesan Cheese
  • Salt & Black Pepper

Instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti in salted boiling water.
  2. Fry the pancetta until crispy.
  3. Whisk eggs with grated Parmesan cheese.
  4. Drain pasta and mix with pancetta.
  5. Remove from heat, then add the egg mixture and mix quickly.
  6. Season with black pepper and serve.

Nutrition Facts (Per Serving)

  • Calories: 450 kcal
  • Protein: 18g
  • Carbs: 55g
  • Fats: 18g
